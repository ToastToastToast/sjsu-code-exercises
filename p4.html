Exercise 6

This exercise uses the javascript libraries p5play, scenemanager, and p5sound. The narrative is a dog that is upset their owner left them so he gets into trouble. The purpose of the sketch is to explore the functionality of the libraries. This exercise taught me how to use p5play animatons and colliders, enabling the ability for a platformer game. I also figured out how the scenemanager operates and how to loop sound.

Project 4

For project 4, aka "The Final Project", I coded a space game. The narrative of the game is aliens are trying to steal the 3rd dimension, transforming the world into a 2d landscape so they can rebuild the world for themselves. The goal is to eliminate the aliens and return the world back to its original state. The narrative plays on the idea of skeumorphism and Flat Design graphics by incoporating the idea into the narrative of the story. The libraries used in this project are p5play, p5.sound, and scenemanager. Sounds and real life imagery are from pixabay and ArgoFox Youtube Channel.
